Graphic Design PORTFOLIO ABOUT

During my internship, A major part of my role was to create new graphic assets of scientific models, workflows, flyers, and other diagrams, as well as coming up with creative ways to illustrate specific concepts. The process would involve several rounds of feedback from scientists, the marketing team, and stakeholders.

Highlights

Infographic


  • The scientists and marketing team at LakePharma wanted to create an informative and visually appealing infographic that could let their clients know what considerations they took in when selecting a rodent host.
  • I was provided only a word document with copy, and I used Adobe InDesign to organize the content and create the infographic. I used Illustrator to create the images of the mice.
  • I created a landing page on HubSpot to publish the final version of the infographic which was then used in a major email marketing campaign to acquire new potential clients.
  • Overall, the infographic landing page campagin had a successful conversion rate of 20%.
  • Landing page address: Rodent Considerations Infographic Landing Page

Email Signatures


  • I designed the layout for a couple of email signatures using Adobe InDesign, so that employees of the company could regularly update their signatures with graphics promoting the most recent company developments or events.
