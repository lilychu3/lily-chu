PHI SIGMA LECTURE SOCIETY

WEBMISTRESS

MAY 2018 - PRESENT

After joining Wellesley College's Alpha Phi Sigma Lecture Society during my first year, I was elected as Webmistress, and still hold this position today. As Webmistress, I am tasked with maintaining and updating the website. The main functions of the website are to display profiles of our members, information about applying to the society and details for upcoming events, and promoting past and future lectures.


Goal: Maintain website and implement improvements based on member and student feedback. Keep site regularly updated with new information and content.

Web Design and Development

  • The Webmistress before me had already created a version of the website using bootstrap. When I inherited the codebase, I had to learn the code structure and build on top of it with limited documentation.
  • I worked on updating the website's functionalities and designs to be more user-focused and relevant.
  • Currently, I am working to further develop the Lecture and Alumnae pages.
