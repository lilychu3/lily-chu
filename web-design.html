Web Design PORTFOLIO ABOUT

At my internship with LakePharma, a B2B pharmaceutical company, I worked with a team of scientists and marketing professionals to develop a website and brand for Hybridoma.com. Hybridoma.com is a micro-site off of the the main LakePharma company website. Its purpose is to promote and educate potential clients on LakePharma's hybridoma services.


Link Address: hybridoma.com

Highlights

Logo Design


  • I designed the new Hybridoma logo in one month, working through many iterations of design feedback from my team members and executive stakeholders.
  • My main takeaway during the design process was learning how to negotiate and compromise on various design directions based on brand constraints such as needing to use Muli or Proxima Nova as the logo type. You can see some of the design explorations below.

Action Plan


  • To be successful on this project, I had to gain domain knowledge about the pharmeceutical industry through user research and working with subject matter experts.
  • As a PM for this project, I created a project plan and Gantt chart to map out timelines for our team in order to keep us on track.
  • Throughout the development process, which took about three months, I met with my team bi-weekly to go over our progress according to the action plan.

Web Development


  • I am the solo developer of hybridoma.com, using Wordpress.
  • I created all designs, illustrations, and graphics using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.
  • I created the animation on the front page by combining various Adobe Stock videos to create the desired cell movement and effect.
  • Currently, I am working on implementing responsive design for mobile devices and improve website performance
