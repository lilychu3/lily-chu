LAKEPHARMA, INC.

DESIGN AND MARKETING INTERNSHIP

JUN 2019 - PRESENT

At my internship with LakePharma, a B2B pharmaceutical company, I worked with a team of scientists and marketing professionals to develop a website and brand for Hybridoma.com. Hybridoma.com is a micro-site off of the the main LakePharma company website. Visit the link at hybridoma.com


Goal: Promote and educate potential clients on LakePharma's hybridoma services with a new website brand for hybridoma.com.

Brand Design

I designed the new Hybridoma and PentaMice logos, working through many iterations of design feedback from my team members and executive stakeholders.


Some takeaways during the design process:

  • Learning how to negotiate and compromise on various design directions based on existing brand constraints, such as using Muli or Proxima Nova as the logo type
  • Logo design is about iterating through and improving many versions of a design based on different preferences of multiple stakeholders

Responsibilities: PM, Design, Developer

  • Managed project timeline and priorities using a gantt chart
  • Created designs, illustrations, and graphics using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, and homepage animation using Adobe Premiere and After Effects
  • Solo developer of hybridoma.com, using Wordpress, HTML, and CSS for desktop and mobile

Graphic Design

Beyond designing and implementing hybridoma.com, I also created several forms of graphic content.

Infographics
Flyer Layouts

Icons
  • A major part of my role was to create new graphic assets of scientific models, workflows, flyers, and other diagrams, as well as coming up with creative ways to illustrate specific concepts.

Motion Design
