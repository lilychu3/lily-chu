After joining Wellesley College's Lecture Society during my first year, I was elected as Webmistress, and still hold this position today. This means I am tasked with maintaining and updating the website. The main functions of the website are to display who the members of the society are, information about applying to the society and details for upcoming events, and promoting past and future lectures.
