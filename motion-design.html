Motion Design PORTFOLIO ABOUT

At LakePharma, I had the opportunity to create various types of motion projects. I produced a promotional video featuring LakePharma's Protein Chemistry Group, and created a LakePharma logo animation to be used for all future video introductions. Currently, I am creating my second promotional video featuring LakePharma's Hopkinton site.

Promotional Video: Protein Chemistry Group

  • Although I was limited by the types of equipment and resources I had access to, I was able to correct any filming and videography issues through post-production editing.
  • I edited the footage and put together the video using Adobe Premiere Pro.
